# ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot – Agile Pitch

## Problem Statement
The current ASSCAT student portal is menu-based and difficult to navigate. Students often struggle to find important services such as checking clearance, viewing grades, or accessing announcements. This results in wasted time, confusion, and poor user experience.

## Industry 5.0 Justification
The ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot follows Industry 5.0 principles by focusing on human-centered technology. Instead of replacing students or staff, the AI assists them by simplifying tasks, providing instant answers, and guiding users through processes. It empowers students by making information more accessible and reducing stress in using digital systems.

## Agile Requirements (User Stories)

1. As a student, I want an AI chat assistant, so that I can quickly ask about my grades, clearance, or schedule without navigating menus.

2. As a student, I want voice input support, so that I can interact with the system faster and more naturally.

3. As a student, I want the AI to work even with limited internet, so that I can still access important information during connectivity issues.
